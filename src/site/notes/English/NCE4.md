## Lesson 6

### New words and expressions

- goodwill
	- exude goodwill 流露出善意
	- promote goodwill
	- create and strengthen goodwill
	- goodwill games 友好运动会
	- goodwill visit / goodwill tour
		- President Obama is on a Middle East goodwill tour
		- President Xi Jinping is on a three-day official goodwill visit to Vietnam.
		- official visit 
		- official goodwill visit
		- informal visit
		- private visit
		- state visit

- inclination
	- have an inclination to do sth.
	- incline 倾斜，使某人倾向于 
		- be inclined to do sth.
			- He is inclined to lose his temper
	- tends to do sth. /  have the tendency to do sth. / be apt / prone to do sth. 往往，常常，倾向于

- contest（通常是有裁判组打分的比赛）
	- a speech / beauty / dance contest
	- game / match（对抗性比赛）
		- a boxing / football / volleyball game / match
	- games 运动会
		- the Olympic Games
	- race(竞速比赛)
		- a car / horse / swimming race
	- open 比赛（公开赛）
	- tournament 锦标赛 / 系列赛
	- championship 冠军赛

- deduce
	- deduce A from B
		- deduce effect from cause
	- deduction 推理、推断、推理结论
	- conclude A from B
		- conclusion
	-  infer A from B
		- inference 推理，推出的结论

- patriotism
	- "patr"="pater": father
		- patriot 爱国者
		- patriotic
			- patriotic songs / education
		- compatriot 同胞
		- patriarchy 父权制
		- patricide 弑父罪
		- paternal 父亲的
			- paternal affection 父爱
		- paternity 父亲的身份
			- He is on paternity leave 修产假
	- “matr"="mater":mother
		- matriarchy：母系社会、母权制
		- matricide 弑母罪
		- maternal 
			- maternal affection
		- maternity
			- She is on maternity leave

- disgrace
	- The dishonest minister was publicly disgraced
	- shame
	- humiliate 使丢脸 / 使出丑
	- bring shame on / bring disgrace on
		- His behavior disgraced / shamed / humiliated / brought shame on his family
	- show sb. up 使出丑（让同行的人丢脸，出丑）
		- Mary's boyfriend showed her up by falling asleep at the concert

- mimic warfare
	- chemical warfare
	- nuclear warfare
	- germ warfare
	- guerrilla warfare 游击战
	- mimic 模拟的,模仿的
		- mimic coloring 模拟色
		- mime 哑剧

### article

- I am always amazed when I hear people saying **that** sport creates goodwill between the nations, and **that** if only the common peoples of the world could meet one another at football or cricket, they would have no inclination to meet on the battlefield.
	- amaze sb.(多强调令人疑惑不解的)
		- surprise sb(使用面更广)
		- astonish sb.
		- astound / dumbfound sb.
	- if only 如果 / 要是
		- If only I were as clever as you are!
	- only if: 只有... 才...
		- She will go only if Peter goes
	- peoples 民族

- Even if one didn't know from concrete examples (the 1936 Olympic Games, for instance) that international sporting contests lead to orgies of hatred, one could deduce it from general principles.
	- even if 即使...（假设）
	- even though... 尽管...（已经发生的事实）
		- **Even if** you dislike **ancient monuments**（古迹）, Warwick Castle is worth a visit.
		- **Even though** you dislike ancient monuments, Warwick Castle is worth a visit.
	- solid 具体的 / 确凿的
		- solid / concrete examples / conditions
		- abstract 抽象的
	- orgies of..../an orgy of 过度的、疯狂的
		- orgies of hatred
		- an orgy of killing
		- an orgy of spending before Christmas
	- binge / spree
		- He went on a drinking binge over the weekend
		- The girls went on a shopping spree on Saturday

-  Nearly all the sports practised nowadays are competitive.
	- pracitise / practise 经常做，从事
	- practice n. 从事，习俗

-  You play to win, and the game has little meaning unless you do your utmost to win.
	- do one's utmost to do 
		- do one's best to do sth.
		- go to  great lengths to do sth
		- endeavor / endeavour to do
		- spare no effort to do sth.
		- go all out to do sth
		- give one's all to do sth

- On the village green, where you pick up sides and no feeling of local patriotism is involved, it is possible to play simply for the fun and exercise: but as soon as the question of prestige arises, as soon as you feel that you and some larger unit will be disgraced if you lose, the most savage combative instincts are aroused.
